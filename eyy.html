About Us

We are a group of second-year Bachelor of Technical-Vocational Teacher Education (BTVTED) students dedicated to building strong foundations in both theory and hands-on skills. As future educators in the technical-vocational field, we value collaboration, continuous learning, and the development of practical competencies that will prepare us for real-world teaching environments. Our group works together to support each other academically and professionally as we strive toward excellence.

Our Background

Our group is composed of BTVTED students currently in our second year of college. United by our goal of becoming effective and skilled technical-vocational educators, we actively participate in academic tasks, practical training, and collaborative projects. Through our shared efforts, we aim to strengthen our abilities, broaden our knowledge, and uphold the values needed in the teaching profession.

Our Mission

To enhance our competence as future technical-vocational educators by engaging in meaningful learning opportunities, fostering teamwork, and demonstrating professionalism, integrity, and dedication to teaching and community involvement.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a group of highly skilled, values-driven, and service-oriented future educators who will contribute to the growth of technical-vocational education and inspire positive change in the communities we serve.
